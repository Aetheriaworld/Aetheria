THE DOMAINS

The Ether: A Domain of light beings, angels, and deities. Powered by multiple suns. Inhabitants: Eths

Earthki: The most "normal" domain, resembling Earth. Earthy beings and animals reside here. Inhabitants: Earthkin

Cosmetica: A domain of stars, weather, and celestial beings. Inhabitants include humanoids formed by/personification of ; the sun or moon, entities born from asteroids and space debris etc
Inhabitants: Cosmetican

THE OUTLAWS

Outlaws are humanoid world cores that dwell within their specific world region or faction. An outlaw is often referred to with the pronoun â€œit,â€ as they are technically considered objects.

Each outlaw has a distinctive personality and theme, and they influence the world around them.

They are personifications of Mother Nature you could say.

For example, if an outlaw is sad, it rains; if they are happy, the sun shines. Outlaws require protection at all costs, 24/7.
If an outlaw is harmed, part of the world also breaks and loses pieces of itself. Many outlaws are treated as royalty, with the Highleader faction serving as their main guardian.
