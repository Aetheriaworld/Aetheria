Angels who partake in the bourgeoisie class hold prestigious positions: doctors, scientists, architects, dukes, landowners, and lords. They often serve also as the Vita's advisors and law enforcer, though one must be exceptionally trusted and loyal to attain such a role.

‚ú¶ The common angel
The common angel is a normal citizen in Aetheria, their lives are pretty average. They most of the time work on farms, or in carpenter shops, post offices, small blacksmiths, or as street market sellers. They do not make much, but it is enough to live on.





However, a first-class angel can work themselves to the top if they work very, very hard.

You can recognize a common angel by the fact that they have two wings and no less than just normal two arms.

THE OUTLAWS

Outlaws are humanoid world cores that dwell within their specific world region or faction. An outlaw is often referred to with the pronoun ‚Äúit,‚Äù as they are technically considered objects.

Each outlaw has a distinctive personality and theme, and they influence the world around them.
